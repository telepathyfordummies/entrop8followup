Objectives

Follow up on Entropy

By the end of this follow up, players should be able to:

  • Distinguish between microstates and macrostates.
  • Relate entropy to the number of possible microstates.
  • Explain why high-entropy states are more probably than low-entropy states.
  • Apply statistical reasoning to physical systems.
  • Describe how probability contributes to the concept of heat death.
  • Understand why we cannot go back.
